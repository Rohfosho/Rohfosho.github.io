Clubs

Join or create different clubs.

Through our new unique user interface you can create a club and share it with your members instantly. The club is also private, only allowing members with a customized code to join. This provides security while maintaining the ease of using the app.

News

Ability to Check the Latest ews updates.

With our new news feed, you can check on updates and information from each club in an instant. This will allow members to be updated on the most current events and news.

Events

Ability to Create Events.

Our new event creator allows the admin to pick a date and location for each event. Also, when the event is created, each member will be notified via e-mail instantly. Members also have an option of RSVP, allowing the admins to know who can attend ahead of time. This will keep both members and admins updated constantly.

Attendance

Take and track attendance.

With our never-before-seen method, taking attendance has never been easier. With only a few clicks, an excel spreadsheet containing of the attendance of each member for every event can be created. This feature is only allowed to admins of the club, allowing the admins to withhold sensitive information.

Email Notifications

Notify your members immediately.

With our new email notifications, your members will receive instant notifications of the latest events and news. This will allow constant communication and collaboration between members and admins.

Push Notifications.

Even faster notification.

Once push notifications are implemented, communication with your members will seem effortless. They will be notified through their phones instantly and the latest news and events will be at the palm of their hands.
