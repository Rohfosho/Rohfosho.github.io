MailSweep

I helped build Mailsweep at HackRU 2014-F. We ended up winning the SendGrid API Prize. Mailsweep is an email intrusion detection system. User signs up on our website and we walk them through set up. It's easy as 1, 2, 3! If we detect that your email may have been compromised, we shoot you a text at the number you provide and verify during sign up.
